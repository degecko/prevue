Targets
You can choose which elements this extension will be able to open. URLs, images, or both.
Type of URLs
Internal links are those belonging to the same website. External links are URLs pointing to different websites.
Open Position
"Opposite the Cursor" means that the popup will be open on the left side if your cursor is on the right side of the screen, and vice-versa.
Open Animation
Display URL Bar
URL Bar Position
Trigger Keys
Trigger Actions
Pressing a Triggered Key will open the previously touched/hovered URL/image.
Holding a Trigger Key and moving the cursor over a URL/image will open it.
Dragging & dropping a URL/image anywhere on the page will open it.
Drag Trigger delays
Open Delay
Release Delay
The Open Delay controls how much the trigger waits until it considers this as a valid interaction. This should be more than 0 to avoid accidental triggers. But not too large, as it won't trigger at all. The Release Delay limits the amount of time it waits for you to release the mouse (drop). This should be less than 1 second to avoid this triggering when you're actually trying to drag and drop a link. Default: 50 to 400 ms; 1 second = 1000 ms
Popup Closing Actions
Pressing Escape will close the opened link.
Clicking outside the URL/image Prevue popup will close the popup.
Scrolling outside the URL/image Prevue popup will close the popup.
Width
You can manually specify how wide the Prevue Popup should be, or you can drag it to the desired size.

Your settings are being saved instantly in your browser's memory. If you're using Chrome Sync, they will also be synced across your devices.
